Desert Survival Solution

Items I would choose:

  1. 1 Magnetic Compass
  2. 15 Gallons of water
  3. 55 Mini bags of peanuts
  4. 3 Airplane blankets
  5. 1 First aid kit

My Solution:
In this situation, I would use the compass to walk in the direction of the nearest city or settlement. On the journey towards the nearest city, I would be sure to cober my skin with the airplane blankets in order to avoid sun poisoning/sunburn. On our journey, we would survive on the 15 gallons of water and 55 bags of peanuts. In case of emergency/injury, I would be sure to keep the salvaged first aid kit on hand.
